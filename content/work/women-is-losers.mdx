SXSW OFFICIAL SELECTION. A POWERFUL STORY OF RESILIENCE SET AGAINST THE BACKDROP OF 1960S SAN FRANCISCO.

AS PRODUCTION MANAGER AND TRANSPORTATION COORDINATOR, I ENSURED 'WOMEN IS LOSERS' MOVED FROM SCRIPT TO SCREEN WITHOUT MISSING A FRAME. I MANAGED DAILY OPERATIONS, BUDGET OVERSIGHT, AND CREW COORDINATION WHILE NAVIGATING THE COMPLEX LOGISTICS OF SHOOTING A PERIOD FILM ACROSS SAN FRANCISCO'S HISTORIC NEIGHBORHOODS.

THE FILM PREMIERED AT SXSW AND BECAME A TIMELY CONVERSATION STARTER ON WOMEN'S RIGHTS AND REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH, EARNING CRITICAL ACCLAIM AND STREAMING DISTRIBUTION ON HBO MAX.
